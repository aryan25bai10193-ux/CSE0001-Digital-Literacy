Social Media Do's and Don'ts for Students

Do's:
1. Use respectful and polite language in posts and comments.
2. Think before posting any content online.
3. Keep your profile professional and appropriate.
4. Verify information before sharing it.
5. Use privacy settings to protect personal information.

Don'ts:
1. Do not share personal or sensitive information publicly.
2. Avoid posting offensive or inappropriate content.
3. Do not engage in cyberbullying or online arguments.
4. Avoid clicking or sharing suspicious links.
5. Do not spread misinformation or fake news.
