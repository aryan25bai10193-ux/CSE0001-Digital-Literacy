Stay Safe Online – Prevention Checklist
1. Never share OTP, PIN, or passwords with anyone.
2. Don’t approve any UPI payment request from an unknown person.
3. Always verify links before clicking on them.
4. Use strong and unique passwords for all accounts.
5. Enable two-factor authentication wherever possible.
6. Avoid using public Wi-Fi for banking or making transactions.
7. Keep your phone and apps updated regularly.
8. Don’t trust unknown calls claiming to be from banks.

**UPI / Financial Safety Tips:**
9. Remember: You do not need to enter a PIN to receive money.
10. Always check the receiver's UPI ID before sending money.

**Report Cybercrime:**
- National Cyber Crime Portal: https://cybercrime.gov.in
- Helpline Number: 1930
